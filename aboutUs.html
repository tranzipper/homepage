About Us

At TranZipper Marketplace, we connect users to skilled professionals and business owners across Nigeria, making it fast and easy to find the services you need. Our platform enables you to access quality services without the high costs of renting physical shops, helping professionals save money and focus on their craft. By bridging the gap between service providers and customers, TranZipper reduces unemployment, offering opportunities to artisans and entrepreneurs nationwide. From bustling cities to remote towns, we serve everyone, ensuring reliable service connections wherever you are, whenever you need them.

OUR STORY

TranZipper Marketplace was born out of a real-life challenge faced by our founder, JP Layefa, in 2018. Layefa found himself struggling financially, despite being a certified photographer with a digital camera. The biggest hurdle? He didn’t have a physical shop to attract customers. In search of a solution, Layefa turned to Google, hoping to find an app that could connect him with potential clients. To his surprise, there was no such platform in Nigeria. Disappointed but determined, he continued to think about how he could bridge this gap. One day, while dressed in his Ankara attire, Layefa heard music coming from a nearby compound. Curious, he walked over, only to find that the celebration—a naming ceremony—was about to be postponed because they couldn’t find a photographer. All the local photographers were already booked. Sensing an opportunity, Layefa quickly fetched his camera, and the event went on as planned. Not only was he paid for his services, but he also enjoyed a plate of delicious Nigerian Jollof. That experience sparked an idea. If he could solve this problem for himself, why not create a platform that could help others too? From that moment, Layefa began developing the TranZipper Marketplace, focusing on user experience and making it easier for professionals and business owners to connect with customers, without the need for a physical shop. Today, TranZipper is a thriving marketplace, helping to reduce unemployment, save money on rent, and connect people across Nigeria to the services they need—quickly and efficiently.

The Founder

JPLayefa

The visionary founder and Chief Executive of TranZipper Technologies, has been driven by a deep commitment to building a safe and seamless ecosystem for Nigerians. His journey began at the age of 15 when his father, recognizing the value of practical skills, enrolled him in a two-year computer training program. This early exposure to technology laid the foundation for his future endeavors. At 19, Layefa’s father further nurtured his potential by enrolling him in photography training, instilling in him the importance of handiwork. With a background in Economics and Political Science, Layefa understands the challenges faced by everyday citizens and is dedicated to making life easier for them. His mission extends beyond technology; he is passionate about empowering individuals to learn valuable skills, encouraging them to pursue handiwork as a means of survival and success. Layefa has devoted his time and energy to creating opportunities for people to earn a living and support their families. TranZipper is a testament to his belief in a better choice for the future—one where creative minds can huddle, collaborate, and build a self-sustaining community. Under his leadership, TranZipper has become a safe haven for professionals, artisans, and business owners to flourish in their craft, transforming the marketplace and impacting lives across Nigeria.

THE CHIEF MARKETING OFFICER

Yaya Sodiq

Yaya Sodiq has been instrumental in the growth and success of TranZipper Marketplace. He began his career working alongside his father in the door-building business in Epe, Lagos, where he developed a strong work ethic and an entrepreneurial spirit. Seeking to expand his knowledge, studied Cultural and Creative Arts and Social Studies in MOCPED, Epe, and later furthered his education at the University of Ibadan. His diverse background, which includes featuring in several television advertisements, reflects his versatility and commitment. As the Chief Marketing Officer of TranZipper, Yaya has been the backbone of the company’s success, contributing greatly to its strategic growth and market presence. His unwavering dedication and efforts have helped establish TranZipper as a thriving platform, making him an essential part of its continued success.
